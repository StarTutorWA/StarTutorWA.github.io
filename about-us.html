Welcome to our Institute

StarTutor is founded by four highly motivated college students, who are passionate about teaching K-12 students and giving back to the community. In July 2020, StarTutor launched its first summer tutoring program on Zoom, including SAT/ACT prep and IB Math/Physics lessons, and quickly gained popularity among parents and students.

As a team, we value our students' experiences and strive to provide one of the best tutoring services.
