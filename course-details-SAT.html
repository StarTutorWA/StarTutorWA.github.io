Objectives

This 15 hour private course covers the strategies and tips on SAT test-taking as well as sample practice tests, out of class help, and content help. The sessions will include teaching SAT English and Math content and test taking strategies, reviewing the homework and practice tests, and answering any questions. A total of 6 practice tests will be completed, totalling to approximately 18 hours of test taking practice. The schedule is to meet twice a week for a total of 5 weeks, but the time and dates are flexible and can be negotiated depending on personal preference.
